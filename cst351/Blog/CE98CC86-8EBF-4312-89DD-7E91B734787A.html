Design Assignment 10
 
This exercise focuses on how designers can use different techniques to enhance meaning. We will explore methods using collage, Photoshop filters, and cropping/placement within the frame.  Begin by using your photograph taken on the first day of class. (I used an alternate photograph.) You may add other images to the composition by you can not alter the facial expression.
