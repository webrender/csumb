Critique Assignment 5
 
Find examples of effective and ineffective navigation systems on the web.  For each component provide a narrative description and screenshots.  Remember that design is a process that reflects choice-making.  How successful are their choices?
 
 
Tuesday, October 10, 2006
Example 1: GOOD navigation:  MTV.com - i know that i have used this site as a subject in a past critique, but i am going to link to it again because it is such a perfect example of clear navigation and design in a page.  All of the links are clearly available at the top of the page, and the links which MTV want you to notice are colored, or have icons with them.  The navigation is thorough without being overwhelming to new visitors, and the color scheme brings the entire site together.
Example 2:  BAD navigation:  United Nuclear - this site is a great example of horrrible design.  The entire page looks like a link listing, which it essentially is.  While the navigation could be cleaned up into subpages, or menus, it lays scrolled out on the main page, with even more links to products to the right hand site.  The color scheme is not hard to read, but certainly isnt pleasing to the eye.
