Critique Assignment 2
 
Using color as the exclusive factor compare and contrast the communication effectiveness of two homepages.Example 1 should demonstrate effective use of color. Example 2 should illustrate ineffective use of color.
 
 
Tuesday, September 12, 2006
Example 1: Sprint.com - Sprint recently redesigned its image - and logo - after merging with Nextel to become one of the largest American cellular companies.  The site makes strong use of yellow, the primary color in their image, and black.  The two contrast very well, and the corporate website makes good use of color as visual aids on the site.  The top navigation bars are easy to see because of the black-on-yellow text, and the site is bright without being distracting or overwhelming.
Example 2: google.com - Google is essentially a household name at this time - a noun, and a verb, a word which has become synonymous with search.  However, this doesn’t correct the fact that they make little use of color theory on their site and in their searches.  All the links are the default blue color, which can confuse users looking for navigation. While the search is clearly laid out, there is little color to help point the user to other tools or options available to them.
