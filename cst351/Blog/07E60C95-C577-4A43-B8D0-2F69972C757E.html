Self Assessment 3
 
Design Process:  After doing sketches for a few of our assignments, I am actually starting to think that i may not get ideas best from doing sketches.  I find that when I am trying to start a project, it helps me more if i just start out with a blank page on my computer.  I have grown so used to drawing and designing with computer tools that drawing naturally that it is actually harder for me to begin a design using my computer than it is to draw it out first.
 
Degree of Difficulty:  The degree of difficulty for this assignment was still pretty rudimentary, as many of these techniques and ideas have already been reviewed in other classes.  I found it hard to work with plain, serif fonts - i found it hard to make such bland fonts noticeable and exciting.  However, i was pretty comfortable working with the different fonts in the assignment.
 
Technique and Production:  I think that i was pretty successful in communicating the objectives of this assignment.  I think that previous to this week in class, i knew the ideas of kerning, orphans and widows etc., but i did not know what they were called and how to address them.  I am much more comfortable with these ideas now, and feel that i could use the guidelines for these design aids properly in future assignments.
 
Reflection:  After completing this week’s work, i feel that i have grown more competent with the ideas of design in regards to types and typefaces, and how to use design in typefaces.
