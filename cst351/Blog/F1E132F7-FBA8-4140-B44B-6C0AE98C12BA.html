Self Assessment 4
 
Design Process:  I think that i found this assignment the most interesting and entertaining of all, so far.  I have always been involved in photography as a hobby, and to focus on learning contrast and how to design with photos had been something i have looked forward to learning more about at CSUMB.  Because i was passionate about this assignment, i found the work especially entertaining, and so while it may have been difficult to find and arrange the right images, i enjoyed doing the assignment.  
 
Degree of Difficulty:  I was really comfortable in this assignment going past my “comfort level”  in this assignment - the format of the work made it so i could really go past what i normally considered in design, and try some unusual techniques in selecting my pictures and croppings.  I think i found the duotone treatment the most difficult to work with, as it used the most complicated arrangement of photographs, and the most editing in an image editor.  
 
Technique and Production:  As stated earlier, I really enjoyed this assignment, and feel that in the end, my solutions worked really well - I’m pretty familiar with photoshop, and i feel that using it in this assignment really helped me to arrange the images the way i had imagined them.
 
Reflection:  After completing this week’s work, i feel that i have gotten more familiar with arranging photographs to convey certain ideas or emphasize certain elements, through different methods such as hue or size.
