Critique Assignment 4
 
Find two examples of photographic experimentation and quality on the web.  
 
Self note:  It was harder to find websites for this critique than the other critiques we have done in the past - i think the reason for this being that websites do not include intensive graphics or photography - most of the time this ends up being a bandwidth hog.  As a result, the pages i found, while they display some examples,, have relatively traditional photography in them.
 
 
Tuesday, October 3, 2006
Example 1: Nokia Europe - this site is a very graphics intensive website, and makes heavy use of flash and javascript.  Each cell phone in the site has a small mini flash page dedicated to it and it’s features.  The flash projects include photography of people using the phones, to make the phones appeal to their target demographic more.
Example 2: wired.com - the Wired website incorporates lots of graphics, but is not as reliant on flash as Nokia’s site.  The site keeps a “rounded” theme in its images, cropping the thumbnails to match the scheme.  The result is an easy to read, flowing site that incorporates lots of informational photography, without being too flashy.
