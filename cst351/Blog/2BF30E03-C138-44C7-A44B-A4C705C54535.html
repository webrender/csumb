Critique 01 - csumb.edu
 
 
Primary Audience:  The primary audience of this page is probably people looking to come to CSUMB - the site doesn’t seem to have a lot of visual aids to assist those already going here.  The main navigational aid (the linkbar at the top) is easy to find, and is laid out in a creative manner.  The other links on the page also clearly convey topics someone interested in coming to the school would find appealing.
 
Secondary Audience:  The main secondary audience would probably be the students and faculty of the school.  This page does not speak to those people.  The links that students access most - housing, registration, etc. - are buried 3 or 4 links down.  For example, to access Banner Web, a student has to click down 4 pages, just to access the login page.  
 
Primary Message (Language):  There really isn’t much language on the homepage to convey;  The site keeps things pretty simple, and most of the language is simply links to more specific sections of the site.
Thursday, August 31, 2006
Primary Message (Design):  The design of the site is intended to match the personality of the Monterey Bay area. The colors chosen represent both the school colors, and the blue of the ocean, and the ocean reference is repeated with the wave design of the navbar.
 
If this design was a person, it would be a quiet, proper boy - think Prince William - moderately good looking on the outside, and friendly to newcomers;  however, on the inside, there are lots of small individual problems stirring.
